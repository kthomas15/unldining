Students at UNL Dining Halls per Meal Period

Waiting in long lines can be aggravating, especially if you're in a hurry. Not all dining halls are the same. Some of them are extremely crowded, while others are fairly reasonable. This application illustrates how many students have scanned into each dining hall at the given meal period in October and November of 2015.


A dining hall may be crowded or nearly empty for various reasons. The location of the hall is a pretty big factor. The meals that are offered that particular day plays an important part as well. Dining halls at UNL often tend to be consistant with their meals. For example, every Tuesday Abel Hall offers their infamous macaroni and cheese bar. This may be why this dining hall is more crowded at this time. To check out the menu, click here.
